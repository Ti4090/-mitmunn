About Us

UMITMUN’25 is an international Model United Nations conference organized by passionate students who believe in the power of diplomacy, debate, and global cooperation. Our mission is to provide a dynamic platform where delegates from different backgrounds can develop their critical thinking, negotiation, and public speaking skills while tackling the most pressing global issues. Taking place in 2025, UMITMUN aims to bring together future leaders, innovators, and changemakers under one roof to simulate the work of the United Nations and promote mutual understanding and collaboration. We are committed to creating an inspiring, educational, and unforgettable MUN experience for every participant.
